    Background of Mark Ingram

    Born on December 21, 1989, in Hackensack, New Jersey USA, Mark Ingram is a prominent American Football player renowned for his role as a running back for the New Orleans Saints in the National Football League (NFL). Ingram made headlines when he clinched the Heisman Trophy in 2009 during his college football career at the University of Alabama before stepping into the professional football arena in 2011.

    Mark Ingram’s wealth report suggests a net worth of $12 million, as of early 2018, primarily amassed through his successful American Football career with an estimated annual salary of over $4 million.

    Growing up in Hackensack, Mark attended Grand Blanc Community High School and later Flint Southwestern Academy. During his time with the Alabama Crimson Tide, he secured the SEC championship in the 2009 – 2010 season and triumphed in the BCS National Championship. Additionally, he became the recipient of the prestigious Heisman Trophy, marking a monumental achievement as the first player from the University of Alabama to claim this accolade. Mark’s field of study was Political Science, but he left before completing his senior year to embark on a professional football career.

    In 2011, the New Orleans Saints handpicked Ingram as the 28th overall in the NFL Draft, marking his entry into professional football. He signed a four-year contract with the Saints, significantly boosting his net worth. His rookie season concluded with 474 rushing yards and five touchdowns. Over the years, Ingram’s career flourished, earning him recognition such as a Pro Bowl selection and a notable rushing record of over 1,000 yards in a single season.

    On the personal front, Mark has been married to fitness model Chelsea since 2017, and they have a daughter.
