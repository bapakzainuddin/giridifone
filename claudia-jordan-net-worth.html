    Claudia Jordan is a widely recognized American actress, radio and television personality, as well as a model. How rich is Claudia Jordan? Born in 1973, in Providence, Rhode Island, Claudia Jordan started her career by participating in beauty pageants held in Rhode Island. Jordan was fairly successful at that, as she won both the Miss Rhode Island Teen USA title in 1990, as well as the Miss Rhode Island USA title 1997. These pageants opened more opportunities for Jordan in the entertainment industry, as she strived to be not only a model, but also an actress.

Even though Claudia Jordan managed to collect some money from her video appearances, as well as increase her net worth, she did not stop at that. Some of other celebrities who were guests on the series include Mark Hoppus, Justin Timberlake, Jessica Alba and Drew Barrymore. Jordan continued making appearances on television screens with the comedy-drama series “Jack & Jill” where she co-starred with Ivan Sergei and Amanda Peet.

The revenue made from “Deal or No Deal” contributed a decent amount of money to Claudia Jordan’s net worth. With her rising popularity, Claudia Jordan started receiving more requests to be featured on various television series and news programs. In addition to that, Claudia Jordan starred in “Celebrity Apprentice”, “That’s So Raven” with Raven Symone, “One on One”, as well as “Dr. Phil” with Phil McGraw.
