    Madeline Kingsburys sister confirms her death by releasing Tiktok video as her ex-boyfriend arres

    31 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Following the Winona Police Department’s press conference on Thursday, Megan Kingsbury, the sister of 27-year-old Madeline Kingsbury who went missing from Winona on March 31, posted a TikTok video providing an update.

    In the video, Megan confirmed that the remains found belong to her sister, Madeline. However, she mentioned that the official cause of death and the state of her remains are yet to be determined, pending information from the medical examiner. Megan emphasized the need for caution and not jeopardizing the investigation by revealing too much information at this stage.

    From the beginning, the family had suspicions about Madeline’s ex-boyfriend, Adam Fravel, and they believe that the right person is now in custody. They express confidence that he will receive the appropriate punishment for his actions.

    The Winona County press conference took place less than 24 hours after the arrest of 29-year-old Adam Fravel on suspicion of murder. Megan previously shared on TikTok her relief at his arrest and the fact that he is no longer a free man.

    The Kingsbury family released a statement ahead of the press conference, expressing their relief that Madeline has been found after months of intensive searching. They thanked law enforcement, first responders, and volunteers for their extraordinary efforts. The family is certain that the correct person has been arrested and expects him to be appropriately charged and convicted. They acknowledge that justice is moving in the right direction and will now focus on mourning as a family and laying Madeline to rest respectfully.
