    Most of the children follow their parent's footsteps. Among others' Bryce Maximus James is also the one who is chasing his dream to become a future NBA star like his father Lebron James.

    The hardworking and talented arising basketball player Bryce was born on June 14, 2007, in Ohio, USA. Bryce is the second-born child of renowned NBA player Lebron James and his beloved wife Savannah Brinson.

    Concerning education, Bryce currently studies at Sierra Canyon School (SC) located in Chatsworth, Los Angeles. Before joining SC, Bryce attended Crossroad School situated in Santa Monica.

    Popular Parents: Lebron & Savannah

    The 13-years-old Bryce is gaining wide media popularity since his birth; all credit goes to his widely famous parents. As mentioned above, Bryce's father Lebron James is the greatest basketball player.

    The 35 aged Lebron currently plays for the Los Angeles Lakers of the National Basketball Association (NBA). The Olympic Gold Medalist, NBA Most Valuable Awards-winning James previously played for professional teams like Cleveland Cavaliers and Miami Heat. 

    On the other side, his mother Savannah James is an American philanthropist and a businesswoman who has launched a mentorship program called Women of Our Future.

    Savannah & Lebron's Strong Marital Bond

    The Los Angles Lakers' player Lebron and Savannah were high school sweethearts who attended St. Vincent-St Mary's High School situated in Akron, Ohio. Ever since they are sharing a good bond as a perfect pair. 

    After dating for several years, on December 31Sierra Canyon, 2011, on the occasion of Lebron's 27th birthday and New Year's Eve party, he proposed his love partner Savannah. She also excitedly accepted his proposal. 

    Afterward, on September 14, 2013, Savannah and Lebron happily tied the wedding knot, in San Diego, California. Mr. and Mrs. James are celebrating their seven-years of marriage anniversary happily. Additionally, they are also parenting their kids jointly. 

    Two Beloved Siblings

    The 5-feet-2-inch (157 cm) tall arising basketballer Bryce Maximus has two siblings, an elder brother LeBron Raymone James Jr, aka Bronny James (b. October 6, 2004) and a younger sister named Zhuri Nova James (b. October 22, 2014).

    Like Bryce, his eldest brother is also pursuing a career as a basketballer. Bronny who also attends Sierra Canyon High School play for the Golf Coast Blue Chips youth basketball team and North Coast Blue Chips. Bronny and the team have won various basketball competitions. 

    Net Worth

    Bryce is struggling hard to pursue a better career in the sports world. Till now he has competed and won several basketball tournaments. At this young age, his spectacular basketball skills have helped him earn wide media attention and huge fans.

    Bryce currently plays for his school Sierra Canyon basketball team. He is not involved in the basketball games professionally, he also has to separate the time for his studies. So, at the moment he is enjoying his parent's net worth. Additionally, Bryce spends his spare time making TikTok videos with his parents and siblings.

    As well all know, Lebron is one of the highest-paid basketball players in NBA history. Lebron owns a total fortune of $480 million which he accumulated from his contract with various professional NBA teams. He also earns from his lifetime endorsement deal with sports brand Nike.

    On the other side, Bryce's mother Savannah has a net worth of $50 million. Additionally, Savannah and her husband Lebron owns three expensive houses in LA and Ohio area. They reportedly spent $23 million, $21 million, and $9 million for each mansion.
