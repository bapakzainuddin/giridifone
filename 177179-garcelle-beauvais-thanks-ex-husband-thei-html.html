    The "Real Housewives of Beverly Hills" star, Garcelle Beauvais, showed her appreciation to the father of her children, Jax Joseph and Jaid Thomas Nilon, in a recent upload.

    The mother of two reveals her pride and invaluable love for her children in a new Instagram post, but also remembered to thank their dad irrespective of their unpleasant past.

    Garcelle Beauvais at the 10th Annual Veuve Clicquot Polo Classic Los Angeles on October 05, 2019 | Photo: Getty Images

    GARCELLE BEAUVAIS'S HEARTWARMING POST

    The delectable reality TV star is thrilled at her boys' growth, and in a bid to celebrate the adolescents as they turn 12-years-old, she scripted the best blend of words to them.

    Motherhood for the African American star is not the only thing that gives her great joy, as she went ahead to show her gratitude towards her ex-husband, Mike Nilon.

    The post featured a variety of pictures, first, showing the boys and their mom as toddlers, then another photo showing the whole family, and lastly, the boys in each other's embrace.

    Garcelle Beauvais at the Kyle & Shahida on September 08, 2019 | Photo: Getty Images

    The photo's caption read:

    "A mother's love is whole, no matter how many times divided! #happybirthday #jaxandjaid #12 you've brought so much love into my life, I'm so proud to be your mom! thx @mcnilon boys, I love you to the moon and back."

    GARCELLE'S PAST RELATIONSHIP WITH MIKE NILON

    This is not the first time the first African American star on the "RHOBH" will be directing a message at her ex-husband.

    Garcelle Beauvais-Nilon and Mike Nilon at Prada in Los Angeles, California, United States | Photo: Getty Images

    According to information garnered by People, the TV star handled the discovery of her former husband's infidelity in such a way that she informed his close associates and close-knit friends about the situation.

    She sent out an email titled "Tiger Woods/ Jesse James / Mike Nilon." Mike's ex-wife dished out her revelation and explained that she felt heartbroken.

    Garcelle continued by bringing her boys to the picture by saying their dad's action was cruel towards them.

    She wrote:

    "...I found out today that MY husband of almost 9yrs have been having an affair for 5yrs with someone in Chicago. I am devastated!!!!... our boys don't deserve this!"

    Even though the parents are separated, they still make it a duty to make their sons' lives fun. Garcelle opened up to Oprah on an episode of "Where Are They Now" about her decision to co-parent with her former husband, regardless of their past.
