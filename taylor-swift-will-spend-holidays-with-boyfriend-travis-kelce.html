    Will they keep the Christmas lights up till January?

    It’s what Swifties want to know after Page Six exclusively reported Tuesday that Taylor Swift and boyfriend Travis Kelce plan to spend the holidays together.

    Kelce’s Chiefs will host the Raiders on Christmas Day next Monday before taking on the Bengals at Arrowhead Stadium on New Year’s Eve, with a source telling the outlet of Swift, “She’ll for sure be at the games.”

    Swift has attended seven of Kelce’s Chiefs games this season, including Sunday’s 27-17 win over the Patriots at Gillette Stadium in Foxborough.

    She was joined by her father, Scott Swift, with the pair taking in the action from a suite with Swift’s new pal, Brittany Mahomes, who is married to Chiefs quarterback Patrick Mahomes.

    Swift and Kelce, both 34, have been seeing each other for months, with the 12-time Grammy winner recently telling Time how their red-hot romance originated.

    “This all started when Travis very adorably put me on blast on his podcast, which I thought was metal as hell,” Swift said of Kelce’s “New Heights” shoutout over the summer, when he revealed how he couldn’t connect with her at the “Eras Tour” in Kansas City.

    “We started hanging out right after that. So we actually had a significant amount of time that no one knew, which I’m grateful for, because we got to get to know each other. By the time I went to that first game, we were a couple.”

    That first game was on Sept. 24, when Kelce’s Chiefs demolished the Bears, 41-10, in front of the Arrowhead Stadium crowd — with Swift watching from a suite.

    “I think some people think that they saw our first date at that game? We would never be psychotic enough to hard launch a first date,” she said.

    Weeks later, the pair stepped out publicly in New York City, where they were photographed holding hands.

    Members of Kelce’s inner circle have noticed a spring in his step, mom Donna Kelce, in particular.

    “He’s happier than I’ve seen him in a long time…. God bless him, he shot for the stars!” Donna told WSJ. Magazine in November.

    The future looks merry and bright for Swift and Kelce, with speculation swirling an engagement could be on the horizon.

    “Scott has been asked for his blessing and has wholeheartedly given it, and Travis has been talking to friends about a ring,” a Kelce source recently shared with Page Six.
