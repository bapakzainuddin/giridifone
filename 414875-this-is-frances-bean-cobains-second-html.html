    Kurt Cobain's daughter, Francis Cobain, tied the knot with Riley Hawk seven years after divorcing her first husband. Intimate details of their wedding remain under wraps.

    Francis Cobain is famously known as the American musician Kurt Cobain's daughter with Courtney Love. Recently, she tied the knot with Tony Hawk's son, Riley Hawk, who also comes from a prominent and wealthy family.

    Frances Cobain during the Moschino x H&M at Pier 36 on October 24, 2018, in New York City. | Source: Getty Images

    Frances and Riley, who have been dating since January 2021, had a low-key wedding. The nuptials took place in Los Angeles on October 7, 2023, soon after they obtained a marriage license in San Diego County in September.

    Musician and former R.E.M. frontman Michael Stipe officiated the wedding. Interestingly, Stipe is also the godfather of the bride.

    Although they don't publicize their relationship as much, Frances has posted glimpses of her thoughts on Instagram that shed light into her life. Previously, she revealed that 2021, the year they got together, had been a year that "brought [her] more into the present moment than [she has] ever been."

    During that time, she steered clear of social media, which helped her spiritual, emotional, and mental health. In 2022, she hoped to "bask in the present moment with reverence, shower the people I am lucky enough to love with more appreciation than words could ever do justice."

    Frances was previously married to The Eeries frontman Isaiah Silva. However, their short-lived marriage began in June 2014 and ended when she filed for divorce in March 2016.

    During their divorce proceedings, Frances claimed she would consider paying Silva in spousal support. However, she did not want her father's reported $450 million estate touched. The former husband and wife ended up having a legal battle for the custody of Kurt's guitar.

    Isaiah Silva alleged that he was given Kurt's millions-worth customized guitar as a gift during their wedding and that he was the rightful owner. Following their divorce, Silva continued to hurt the Cobain family's reputation with several allegations.
