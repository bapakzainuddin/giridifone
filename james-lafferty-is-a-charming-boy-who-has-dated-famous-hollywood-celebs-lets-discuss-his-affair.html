    James is quite a good-looking man!

    One Tree Hill star James Lafferty is quite a good-looking man. He is sure to have attracted a lot of women around the globe, and that has prompted him to be in several relationships.

    Lafferty is an accomplished man not only in his career, but he also boasts an extensive list of girls he dated in the past. So today, we take a look at his dating history and find out if he has someone in his life at the moment.

    Currently, he is engaged to Alexandra Park. His fiance, Alexandra is an Australian actress.

    The Longest relationship between James

    James was in a relationship with Irish actress Eve Hewson for around five years. The two started dating back in 2010.

    The relationship, however, was always kept very private, and thus, the flow of the relationship and also the reason for the demise of their relationship were not disclosed to the fans and the media.

    It was his one of the longest relationships and James does boast a long dating history, and that shows that he is quite the lady’s man.

    James’ second affair

    James also dated the gorgeous actress Shantel VanSanten from 2009-2010. But James and Shantel never advertised the relationship much as they preferred it to keep far from the eyes of the media.

    James’ next affair started on-screen

    Moreover, James also dated his One Tree Hill co-star Sophia Bush from 2008-2009. The couple met back in 2003 on the sets of this same project, and they later dated one another.

    After denying the dating rumors in the initial phase, one source confirmed that the two were indeed dating. The source elaborated in 2008,

    “They’re definitely together,”

    “It’s one of those things where you grow up a lot faster. You’re put in a situation where you’re gonna deal with things right in your face. But I think [Sophia and I] are both professional and mature enough to get through it. And now it’s just easy. We’re just friends. And she’s got James, and James and I are friends. It’s just a little tight group in Wilmington.”
