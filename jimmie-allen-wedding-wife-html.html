    Jimmie Allen and Alexis Gale gave their fans an insight into their stunning wedding. The couple appeared to be overjoyed as they shared beautiful portraits from their ceremony on May 30, 2021.

    The country musician and his bride tied the knot in an intimate ceremony on May 27, 2021, after postponing it because of the pandemic.

    Family and close friends including Tyler Rich and his wife Sabina Gadecki, Chuck Wicks, and Darius Rucker attended the wedding.

    Soon after saying "I do," Gale posted a picture from their wedding photo album on her Instagram Story. And, the couple recently shared more photos from their special day.

    In the first snap shared by the singer, the newly married duo looked amazing as Allen placed his hands on Gale’s hip from the back.

    While the bride looked gorgeous in a deep-neck white dress with pink undertones embellished with stones and feathers, the groom flaunted a daring light pink coat paired with a black shirt and same-colored pants.

    The white/pink flowers picked for the décor, wonderfully blended with the stunning garden and lake in the background.

    The Couple Got Engaged in 2019

    The singer popped the question to Gale in July 2019 at Disney World. She announced her engagement by uploading a photo of her engagement ring along with the charming hashtag #feyonce.

    The couple had planned to marry last year, but the virus outbreak put a stop to their preparations.

    "Yeah, we pushed it back to next year, so I'm excited about that," Allen revealed to People in July 2020. "Hopefully, it'll all be good."

    The duo met through Allen's cousin's wife.

    They are both from Milton, Delaware, and began dating in spring 2019.

    The country performer said that since his wife and he shared the same hometown, he felt like he had a piece of home with him all the time.

    The two quickly became friends and soon started dating. The ‘Best Shot’ singer proposed a few months later.

    The couple shares a 14-month-old daughter, Naomi Bettie, and the musical artist has a 7-year-old son, Aadyn, from his previous relationship.
