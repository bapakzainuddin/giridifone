    Mia Tyler Wiki Biography

    Born Mia Abagale Tallarico on the 22nd December 1978 in Lebanon, New Hampshire USA, she is an actress, plus-size model, and fashion designer, but is perhaps best known to the world as the daughter of the legendary rock singer, Steven Tyler.

    Have you ever wondered how rich Mia Tyler is, as of early 2018? According to authoritative sources, it has been estimated that Mia Tyler’s net worth is as high as $5 million, an amount earned through her successful career, active since the mid-‘90s.

    Mia Tyler Net Worth $5 Million

    The daughter of Steven Tyler and his ex-wife, actress Cyrinda Foxe, who passed away in 2002 after a long battle with a brain tumor, Mia spent her early years on Lake Sunapee in New Hampshire. Her parents divorced when she was nine years old and withher mother, she moved to New York City.

    Mia’s career started when she was only 17 years old, as VJ in the MTV show “House of Style”; soon after she was recruited by Wilhelmina Models, and became a so-called plus-size model, since when she has been featured in a number of fashion magazines, such as Seventeen, Teen People, Vogue, YM, Us among others, which has increased her net worth to a large degree. Furthermore, she has been used as a model by H&M, Lane Bryant and MXM, and has walked on runways of such fashion cities as New York and Paris, further increasing her net worth.

    In 2005 Mia launched a television career, making her debut in VH1’s “Celebrity Fit Club”, and in 2006 appeared in the VH1 reality show “Really Rich Real Estate”. She subsequently served as a judge in the reality show “Pretty Wicked”, aired on the Oxygen network.

    Mia is also an actress; as early as 2000, she made her debut as Laura Simon in the film “A Little Bit of Lipstick”, while in 2002 she was the Body Snatcher in the drama film “People Are Dead”. Furthermore, she played Marsha in the action comedy film “Rush Hour 3” in 2007, starring Jackie Chan, Chris Tucker and Max von Sydow. Additionally, in 2009 Mia was a part of season six of “Hell’s Kitchen” on Fox TV, with all of these engagements contributing to her net worth.

    To speak further of her accomplishments, Mia published her autobiography “Creating Myself” back in 2008, and launched her own clothing line “Revolution 1228” in early 2009, with both projects adding a considerable amount to her wealth.

    When it comes to her personal life, Mia was married to Dave Buckner, a drummer, from 2002 until 2005, and was also engaged to a guitarist Brian Harrah, however, the two split up before taking the final step She now lives with her boyfriend Dan Halen with whom she has a child, a son Axton Joseph, born in May 2017. She is a paternal half-sister to Liv Tyler, Chelsea Tallarico, and Taj Tallarico.
