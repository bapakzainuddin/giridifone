    Mackenzie Frances Ziegler, born on 4 June 2004 in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania USA to Melissa Ziegler-Gisoni and Kurt Ziegler, has a net worth estimated at $2 Million, with a salary of around $600,000. Ziegler initially rose to fame for her appearance in the reality television show ‘Dance Moms’. The series focused on young dancers’ journey to success and Ziegler appeared in a total of 159 episodes. She also ventured into music, releasing the song ‘Shine’ as Mack Z in 2014 and then went on to achieve success with her album ‘Mack Z’. Ziegler has also made a mark in the modeling and acting industries, walking for the Polo Ralph Lauren Runway in New York City and making appearances in movies and television shows.

    Ziegler’s career expanded to dance and music videos, television shows, movies, and even modeling. Besides her on-screen and modeling endeavors, Ziegler and her sister were spokespeople for Clean & Clear cosmetics, and she has also launched a clothing line of activewear inspired by dancing, through retailer Justice. Apart from her professional accomplishments, Ziegler is also known for her charity work, supporting organizations like the Starlight Children’s Foundation, Dancers Against Cancer, and Love Your Melon. She is also active on social media, garnering a significant following on Musical.ly app.

    In terms of personal life, Ziegler’s parents are divorced, and she is currently in 8th grade, being homeschooled while taking online classes. She plans to release a self-help book in 2018. With myriad achievements across different fields, Mackenzie Ziegler has solidified her status as a multi-talented personality.
