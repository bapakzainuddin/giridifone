    SAN ANTONIO, TEXAS: NBA free agent, Bryn Forbes was arrested on Wednesday, February 15 for assaulting his ex-porn star girlfriend, Elsa Jean. Forbes was arrested at a residence in north-central San Antonio. He was taken to the Bexar County Jail, where bail had not been initially set before it was adjusted to $2,500.

    According to a San Antonio police report, Forbes had a verbal argument with his girlfriend, Jean, before striking her several times. Prior to the incident, the couple was reportedly at La Cantera Resort & Spa, and the argument started when they got home. The San Antonio Fire Department and Emergency Medical Services came to Jean's aid after a close friend reported the incident. Forbes has been charged with assault. No attorney has yet been named to speak on Forbes' behalf, and the case remains under investigation.

    Who is Elsa Jean?

    Elsa Jean is a 26-year-old former American adult film actress who is known for her work with the production companies 3rd Degree and Digital Sin. Elsa was only 18 years old when she joined the adult film industry in 2015. At a young age, before starting her career as a porn star, she  started off as a stripper in Washington, DC. She is known by the nicknames Elsa and Molly. According to a report by the DailyMail, Elsa had been living with her NBA boyfriend for several months. Elsa is quite devastated by the whole situation and says that she will be leaving him.

    More about Bryn Forbes

    Bryn Forbes is an NBA free agent. He has played seven seasons in the NBA after going undrafted in 2016. He played his first four seasons with the San Antonio Spurs. Then, in 2021, he joined the Milwaukee Bucks as a free agent but returned to the Spurs in 2022, and he was traded to the Denver Nuggets midway through that season.

    Forbes signed with the Minnesota Timberwolves in July 2022, but they waived him last week to clear a spot after acquiring Mike Conley Jr and Nickeil Alexander-Walker in a trade. He played at Cleveland State and Michigan State before the NBA. Before dating Elsa, he was married to Raelynn Taylor, and now they share the custody of their two children.
